Who develops Sguil?

Sguil is activately developed by a group of professional individuals who use it in real production environments. We are not out there to sell you some slick interface that accomplishes nothing. We believe in the process of Network Security Monitoring and are trying our best to spread the word and make the lives of security analysts easier.

Bamm Visscher is the lead developer and can be contacted directly via bamm at sguil dot net.
